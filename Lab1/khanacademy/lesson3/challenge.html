Recipe #1 Oliver`s Salad

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4-6
Ingredients Quantity
Vegetables 2 kilogram
Mayo 4 table spoons
Sausage or meat 300 grams

Step 1: Boil carrots and potatoes.

Step 2: Boil hard-boiled eggs.

Step 3: Cut eggs, potatoes, sausage, carrots into small cubes of equal size.

Step 4: Slice the cucumbers and squeeze a little so that there is not a lot of liquid in the salad.

Step 5: In the chopped ingredients pour the peas (draining the water from it), chop greens, mix with mayonnaise. Season with salt and pepper. Put in the fridge for 1 hour.

Recipe #2 Stolichnyj Salad

  • Time: 3 hours
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Vegetables 2 kilogram
Apple 2 pcs.
Mayo 4 table spoons
Beef tongue 400 grams

Step 1: Prepare the tongue for the classic Stolichny salad. Wash thoroughly and put it in a pot. We suggest boiling the tongue as a whole, and for the salad use only a part - weighing 400 grams. The rest you can just cut into thin slices and serve as a cold snack. Pour water over the tongue, put it on the fire and bring it to a boil. Skim off the foam. Boil the tongue for 3 hours on low heat. Allow to cool and peel. Cut off the part to be used for salad dressing.

Step 2: Boil hard-boiled eggs.

Step 3: Cut eggs, potatoes, sausage, carrots into small cubes of equal size.

Step 4: Slice the cucumbers and squeeze a little so that there is not a lot of liquid in the salad.

Step 5: In the chopped ingredients pour the peas (draining the water from it), chop greens, mix with mayonnaise. Season with salt and pepper. Put in the fridge for 1 hour.

Source: Gastronom.ru

Recipe #3 Caesar Salad

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Toasted Bread 4 pcs.
Olive oil 3 table spoons
Romano salad 200 grams
Chicken 300 grams
Caesar sauce 300 grams

Step 1: Prepare the dressing for the classic Caesar salad with croutons. Peel the garlic cloves and put them in a mortar. Add anchovy fillets and salt and grind with a pestle until you obtain a puree.

Step 2:Continuing to work with the pestle, thinly pour in olive oil. Then gradually add all the other ingredients of the sauce. As a result, the mortar should form a homogeneous emulsion. Put it in the fridge until you use it.

Step 3: Prepare the croutons for a classic Caesar salad. Cut the crust off the slices of bread, cut the crumb into squares and place on a baking sheet lined with parchment.

Step 4: Drizzle the bread cubes with olive oil. Heat the oven to 180Â°C. Place the tray with the bread cubes in it and bake until golden for 10-15 minutes. Turn them over a couple of times.

Step 5: Wash the lettuce leaves and dry them well on a paper towel. Tear them up and place in a large salad bowl. Pour the dressing over the salad and sprinkle with the cooled croutons. Serve the Caesar salad immediately.

Source: Gastronom.ru
